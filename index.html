The family tree shown above shows the hierarchy of the Trump family, starting with Donald Trump. The next hierarchy hows his three spouses and their offspring. The tree is shown horizontally because the family tree is deeper than it is broad, making it easier to see on our wide screens (with the exception of mobile phones).

We added images of the persons to make it easier to relate the names to our visual memory. The images works as both a tooltip, which triggers a Wikipedia description when hovered, and a hyperlink that allows the user to read more if desired (when the Wikipedia article exists).
